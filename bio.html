About Me


Pino is a passionate and driven student at the University, as well as the founder of the ImperialMC Network.
He has been involved in the field since 2012 and has developed a deep expertise in plugin setup and development
using Java and MySQL as well as setting up and optimizing Minecraft servers and Discord servers.

Due to is passion, perseverance and experience, Pino is able to build secure Minecraft servers from
scratch, ensuring that they are optimized for the best possible performance and gameplay.
His approach to problem-solving is always centered around a commitment to learning and growth, and he is always willing to
go the extra mile to ensure that his clients and customers are satisfied.

His commitment has helped him developing 360° skills necessary to develop a successful server in every aspects.
Pino is also known for his helpful and collaborative approach to work.

He has created guides and tutorials to help others navigate the world of Minecraft servers and has helped
countless people including large networks.
With his skills and expertise, Pino is a valuable asset to any team looking to build high-quality Minecraft and Discord servers.

Whether you're a Minecraft server owner looking to optimize your existing server or building one from scratch,
Pino has the skills and expertise to help.
