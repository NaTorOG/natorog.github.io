FAQs

How much do you charge?

The price is discussed with the client and may vary depending on the service requested, the deadline and the priority assigned.

How can I request your services?

Feel free to join my Discord and open a ticket describing accurately your request.
Remember to answer the survey in order to write in the ticket.

Do you need special permissions?

Depending on your service requested I may need some special permissions for example SSH or an Administration Role in game or on Discord.

Can I trust you?

Trust is one of the hardest things to achieve but to do my job to the fullest I will need your full trust and willingness.
We will be in touch, when needed, during my work and you will be able to monitor me.

Why should I choose you?

Since 2012 I started working with Minecraft and its development side.
Over the years I have developed 360° skills that allow me to have extensive knowledge of all the mechanics behind a Minecraft server.
I am passionate about what I do and I do it giving 100% of me.
If I will not be able to help you with your request I will tell you so you will not lose your time and investment.

Which payment methods do you accept?

Most of the transactions are done using PayPal and Credit/Debit cards.
Additionally it is also possible to pay using Bitcoin, BNB, ETH or BUSD.
