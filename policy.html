Privacy Policy

1 - What data do I keep?

Website

2 - How do I use your data?

To improve my services
I use the data collected to improve my services or offer beneficial offers.

To improve security in my community
I am constantly working to keep our platforms a safe, positive and inclusive place.
I do this by using the data collected to monitor and take action against users and content that violates our
Terms of Service, our Rules and other policies.

To prevent fraud and illegal acts
We use your information to prevent fraud, scams, and illegal acts that may be caused by malicious users so as to ensure the security of my work.

4 - How long do I keep your data?

I retain users' personal data for as long as necessary to achieve my business, ensure the smooth operation of my services and my security so because of that I can after a long period of inactivity provide a cleaning of the collected data.
The data I could arrange to purge are listed in point one of the privacy policy.

 5 - How do I secure your data?

Website
All information sent as part of my services is encrypted, both during transit and at idle.
My site does not use tracking cookies.

Services
During my services I might, depending on what you requested me, need special permissions or access to sensitive sections example during firewalling and administration operations.
In that case, depending on the service requested, I will store your data in an highly encrypted vault using strong AES encryption.
Your data will be deleted at the end of our working partnership.

Discord
Ticket transcripts containing sensitive data will be removed from the transcript I keep in my private channel.

6 - How can I see or delete my data?

Since I only save ticket transcripts, if you have not received it, you can request it.
Transcripts are deleted after a certain amount of time of inactivity, and you will be able to request their deletion after a minimum of 60 days from the end of our working relationship.
